Choclate Chip Cookie 🍪🎂✨

Description

Yummy and delicious Cookies!! freashly baked!! and nice to make on a cozy day! 🍪😋✨

🍪 Ingrediant Time (what you need!) ✨!!

  • Butter 🧈🍪 1 cup (2 sticks)
  • Chocolate chips or take your favorite chocolate bar and cut it up into your liking!
  • All Purpose Flour
  • Brown Sugar 🤎 make sure its fresh!!
  • White Sugar 🤍 !! You will need 1/4 cup of granulated sugar to add the rest of the sweetness to the cookies.
  • Eggs!🥚 one to two large eggs!
  • Vanilla Extract!! The higher the quality of the Vanilla, the better!!
