Chocolate Chip Cookie 🍪🎂✨

Description

Yummy and delicious Cookies!! freashly baked!! and nice to make on a cozy day! 🍪😋✨

🍪 Ingrediant Time (what you need!) ✨!!

  • Butter 🧈🍪 1 cup (2 sticks) MELTED BUTTER 🧈
  • Chocolate chips! 1 Cup! you can buy chocolate chips anywhere! or you take your favorite chocolate bar and cut it up into your liking! Its your call 😜🥛🍫
  • All Purpose Flour! two 1/2 cups!!
  • Brown Sugar 🤎 3/4 make sure its fresh!!
  • White Sugar 🤍 !! You will need 1/4 or 1/2 cups of granulated sugar to add the rest of the sweetness to the cookies.
  • Eggs!🥚 one to two large eggs! and extra yolk if needed!
  • Vanilla Extract!! i'd recommend 2 teaspoons but!! The higher the quality of the Vanilla, the better!!
  • Salt ✨🧂 Just a little bit of salt helps to bring out all of the other flavors in the cookies i recommend 1/2 teaspoon ✨.
  • Nutella!! you will need a total of 3/4 cups!!

    • (Disclaimer❕If you want your cookies to be fluffier i recommend 1 teaspoon of Baking Soda!! Backing soda acts as the leavening agent in the cookie) 🍪😋

🎂 Step Times ✨

  1. Turn on your favorite baking playlist 🎶 your about to make magic! ✨
  2. ‼️WAIT‼️ Before you start, Grab a small tray and line it with parchment paper. Now take your jar of Nutella (aka the good stuff 😋) and use a ice cream to scoop out little nutella circle blobs!! Then drop them onto the tray. Pop that tray in the freezer for about 45 to 1 hour until those blobs are firm and a little forzen 🥶❄️❕
  3. Now lets start!! In one bowl, whisk together flour,(if you want, add baking soda now!!) salt 🧂
  4. Boring but most important part! In another bowl (the main stage),Mix your melted butter 🧈, brown sugar 🧸 and granulated sugar 🧁, UNTIL ITS SMOOTH AND GLOSSY 🫧🎂. Add on tne egg 🥚, extra yolk and your fav vanilla extract! 🍦 Then whisk again until everything's beautifully blended 🧑‍🍳
  5. Now slowly add your dry ingredients into the wet mix!! Stiring until a soft dough forms 🥐 Finally, fold in your chocolate chips 🍪🍫
